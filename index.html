Why haven?

Haven is a free and open-source meditation timer. Unlike other apps, Haven is minimal and easy to use. Our goal is to help you focus on meditation without distractions.

Key Features

  • Simple Design: Clean and uncluttered interface.
  • Installable: Use it offline like a native app.
  • Dark Mode: Calming dark mode for night-time use.
  • Accessible: User-friendly for everyone.
  • Open Source: Haven's code is openly visible for everyone.

How to Use

  1. Install: Add Haven to your home screen from your browser.
  2. Set Timer: Choose your meditation time and chime.
  3. Start: Begin meditating and enjoy the calm.
